Our Story

Where Every Shot Counts

Los Carnales is more than a billiard hall — it's a gathering place. A dimly lit room where conversations flow as freely as the drinks, and the crack of a well-struck cue ball is its own kind of music.

We blend old-school pool hall soul with a curated cocktail program and an atmosphere that keeps you coming back. Whether you're a seasoned player or just here for the vibe, there's a seat — and a stool — with your name on it.
